### 🎲 The Dollar Game: A Wealth Inequality Simulator
Imagine 50 people in a room. In each round, everyone randomly gives one dollar to someone else.
At first glance, it seems totally fair — the money is moving around completely at random.
But over time, you start to see a pattern: a few people end up with most of the money, and the rest are left with almost nothing !
This game shows that even when everyone starts equal and acts randomly, inequality can still emerge naturally.
It's a simple but powerful reminder that fairness in systems isn't always as straightforward as it seems.
